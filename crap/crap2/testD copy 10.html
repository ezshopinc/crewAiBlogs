Mountain Biking Routes That Will Change Your Life

Mountain biking is a sport that is loved worldwide, and for a good reason! The thrill of speeding down a narrow trail with the wind in your face and the stunning beauty of the remote locations that it can take you to, make it one of the most exhilarating ways to get your adrenaline pumping. It also offers a great way to stay fit and get outdoors, whether you’re an avid cyclist or just looking for a new hobby. 


It’s no secret that we love cycling here at Cycle évasion, but mountain biking is a whole other world! The freedom and thrill that mountain biking offers is unmatched. In this blog, we’ll discuss some of the best mountain biking routes around the world, and what makes them so special. 


Introduction to Life-Changing Mountain Biking Trails


Mountain biking is a sport that is loved worldwide, and for a good reason! The thrill of speeding down a narrow trail with the wind in your face and the stunning beauty of the remote locations that it can take you to, make it one of the most exhilarating ways to get your adrenaline pumping. It also offers a great way to stay fit and get outdoors, whether you’re an avid cyclist or just looking for a new hobby. 


The appeal of mountain biking is a simple one, it’s the thrill of the ride! The feeling of wind in your face as you race down a mountain, the challenge of navigating technical terrain, and the reward of breathtaking views make mountain biking the ultimate adventure sport. What sets mountain biking apart from other adventure sports is the degree of freedom it offers. With a relatively low cost to get started and a seemingly endless network of trails worldwide, the opportunities for exploration are boundless. 


Unforgettable North American Mountain Biking Routes


Whistler, Canada: The Ultimate Biking Playground

It seems only fitting to start with our very own backyard, Canada! Whistler is known as the holy grail of mountain biking destinations. In 2006, Whistler was the host of the first ever Cycle évasion store and it has been the ultimate mountain biking destination ever since. It’s not just the fact that it is home to the world’s largest bike park, but also the diversity of its trails, the quality of the ride, and the stunning alpine views that make it so special. 


Moab, Utah: The Red Rock Challenge

Moab is a small town in Utah that is world-famous for its mountain biking. The red rock landscape is otherworldly, with stunning views of the desert, including the iconic Delicate Arch. 


Pisgah National Forest, North Carolina: Lush Green Trails

Pisgah National Forest in North Carolina is a lesser-known mountain biking destination, but it is no less impressive. The east coast is home to some of the best mountain biking in the United States, with a diverse range of trails that offer something for every skill level. 


Epic European Mountain Biking Adventures


The Swiss Alps: Breathtaking High-Altitude Rides

The Swiss Alps are famous for skiing, but they are also home to some of the best mountain biking in the world. In the summer months, the snow melts, and the ski slopes turn into a network of mountain biking trails. The high-altitude trails offer stunning views of the surrounding mountains, and the well-maintained trails make for a thrilling ride. 


French Pyrenees: Thrilling Trails and Scenic Beauty

The Pyrenees mountain range is a lesser-known mountain biking destination, making it a great option for those looking to escape the crowds of the Swiss Alps. The Pyrenees are located along the border of France and Spain, and they are home to a diverse range of trails that cater to every skill level. 


Scottish Highlands: Rugged and Wild Rides

The Scottish Highlands are home to some of the most remote and rugged landscapes in Europe. The mountain biking trails in the Scottish Highlands are not for the faint of heart. The weather is unpredictable, and the trails are rough and technical. However, for those who are up to the challenge, the Scottish Highlands offer some of the most rewarding mountain biking in Europe. 


Exotic Mountain Biking Destinations in Asia


Kathmandu Valley, Nepal: The Himalayan Experience

Nepal is home to the tallest mountains in the world, so it should come as no surprise that it is a great mountain biking destination. The Kathmandu Valley is the most accessible mountain biking destination in Nepal, and it is a great option for those who want to experience the beauty of the Himalayas without the altitude. The trails in the Kathmandu Valley range from easy rides through local villages to challenging singletrack trails that descend into the valley. 


Mt. Fuji, Japan: A Unique Cultural Ride

Japan is another unexpected mountain biking destination. The Mt. Fuji trail is a 100-kilometer trail that circumnavigates the iconic Mt. Fuji. The trail is a mix of gravel roads and singletrack, and it offers stunning views of the mountain. 


Southern Hemisphere's Hidden Gems


Rotorua, New Zealand: Geothermal Wonders and Trails

New Zealand is home to some of the best mountain biking in the world. The small town of Rotorua is located on the North Island of New Zealand, and it is a mountain biking mecca. The Whakarewarewa Forest is home to a diverse range of trails that cater to every skill level. The trails wind through a stunning redwood forest, and they are known for their challenging terrain and technical features. 


Table Mountain, South Africa: A Ride with a View

Cape Town is known for its stunning landscapes, and it is a great destination for outdoor enthusiasts. While Table Mountain is best known for its hiking trails, it is also home to some great mountain biking trails. The trails on Table Mountain offer stunning views of the city and the surrounding coastline, making it a great place to go for a ride and watch the sunset. 


Patagonia, Argentina: Untamed Wilderness

Patagonia is a vast region that spans across both Chile and Argentina. The Andes mountain range runs through Patagonia, making it a great destination for mountain biking. The Carretera Austral is a 1,240-kilometer highway that runs through the remote Aysén region of southern Chile. The highway is mostly unpaved, making it a challenging but rewarding mountain biking destination. The Carretera Austral offers stunning views of the Andes mountains, as well as access to some of the most remote and pristine wilderness in Patagonia. 


Tips for Planning Your Epic Mountain Biking Journey


Invest in a Good Bike and Equipment

Before you set off on your mountain biking adventure, it’s important to make sure that you have the right equipment. Investing in a good bike and equipment is essential for staying safe and having an enjoyable ride. We recommend starting with a visit to Cycle évasion for expert advice and a great selection of bikes and gear from top brands like Santa Cruz, Juliana, and Yeti Cycles. 


Get in Shape

Mountain biking can be a physically demanding sport, especially if you’re planning to ride on challenging trails. We recommend getting in shape before your trip. Start by riding on local trails to get a feel for your bike, and gradually work your way up to more challenging trails. 


Be Prepared for the Elements

The weather can be unpredictable in mountain biking destinations, especially at high altitudes. Make sure that you’re prepared for the elements by bringing a backpack with a waterproof jacket, extra layers, and plenty of water.

Patagonia, Argentina: Untamed Wilderness

Patagonia is a vast region that spans across both Chile and Argentina. The Andes mountain range runs through Patagonia, making it a great destination for mountain biking. The Carretera Austral is a 1,240-kilometer highway that runs through the remote Aysén region of southern Chile. The highway is mostly unpaved, making it a challenging but rewarding mountain biking destination. The Carretera Austral offers stunning views of the Andes mountains, as well as access to some of the most remote and pristine wilderness in Patagonia. 


Tips for Planning Your Epic Mountain Biking Journey


Invest in a Good Bike and Equipment

Before you set off on your mountain biking adventure, it’s important to make sure that you have the right equipment. Investing in a good bike and equipment is essential for staying safe and having an enjoyable ride. We recommend starting with a visit to Cycle évasion for expert advice and a great selection of bikes and gear from top brands like Santa Cruz, Juliana, and Yeti Cycles. 


Get in Shape

Mountain biking can be a physically demanding sport, especially if you’re planning to ride on challenging trails. We recommend getting in shape before your trip. Start by riding on local trails to get a feel for your bike, and gradually work your way up to more challenging trails. 


Be Prepared for the Elements

The weather can be unpredictable in mountain biking destinations, especially at high altitudes. Make sure that you’re prepared for the elements by bringing a backpack with a waterproof jacket, extra layers, and plenty of water.
